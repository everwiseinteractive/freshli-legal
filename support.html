Frequently Asked Questions

Is Freshli free?

Yes. Freshli is free to download and use. Freshli Pro offers optional premium features via a subscription, but the core pantry tracking, expiry alerts, and community sharing are completely free.

Does the AI need internet?

No. Rescue Chef uses Apple's on-device FoundationModels framework. Your pantry data is processed entirely on your iPhone — nothing is sent to any server. It works offline.

Which devices are supported?

Freshli requires iOS 26 or later. The AI Rescue Chef feature requires Apple Intelligence (iPhone 16 and later). Apple Watch complications require watchOS 26.

How do I delete my account?

Open Freshli → Profile → Settings → scroll to the bottom → Delete Account. All your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We use Row Level Security so only you can access your data. We never sell your data or use third-party trackers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Freshli Pro → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of the current billing period.

Can I share food with neighbours?

Yes! Go to the Community tab and tap "New Post" to list surplus food for free collection. You can also browse what neighbours are sharing and claim items near you.

Still need help?

If your question isn't answered above, email us at support@freshli.app with a description of your issue. Include your device model and iOS version so we can help faster.

Report a Bug

Found something that doesn't look right? Email support@freshli.app with the subject line "Bug Report" and include:

  • What you were doing when the issue occurred
  • What you expected to happen vs what actually happened
  • A screenshot if possible
  • Your iPhone model and iOS version
