Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add items to my pantry?

You can add items manually by tapping the "+" button on the home screen, scan receipts using the camera icon, or use Siri by saying "Add item in Freshli." The Smart Add feature also suggests items based on common groceries.

How do expiry notifications work?

Freshli sends you push notifications when items are approaching their expiry date. You can customize notification timing (1 day, 3 days, 1 week before) in Settings > Notifications. The app also highlights items on your dashboard sorted by urgency.

What are Freshli Circles?

Freshli Circles are community groups where you can share surplus food with neighbors, coordinate grocery runs, and plan meals together. You can create a circle, invite members, and use SharePlay for real-time collaboration. Your exact location is always privacy-protected.

Is my location shared with other users?

Your privacy is our priority. When sharing food through Circles, we only show an approximate location (offset by approximately 200 meters). Your exact address is never revealed to other users. You can disable location sharing entirely in Settings > Privacy.

How does Recipe Rescue work?

Recipe Rescue analyzes your pantry inventory and suggests recipes that use ingredients you already have, prioritizing items closest to expiry. Recipes are filtered by your dietary preferences and can be saved to your favorites for easy access.

How do I track my food waste impact?

Your Impact Dashboard (accessible from the main tab bar) shows your food waste statistics including items saved, money preserved, CO2 emissions prevented, and your streak. You earn achievements and milestones as you reduce waste over time.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to Settings > Privacy > Export My Data. You'll receive a complete export of your pantry items, recipes, impact metrics, and account data in a standard format within 24 hours.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account in Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data within 30 days. You can also request deletion by emailing privacy@freshli.app. For more details, see our Privacy Choices page.

What devices are supported?

Freshli requires iOS 18.0 or later and is optimized for all iPhone models including iPhone 17 Pro Max. Some features like SharePlay require iOS 18+ and an active internet connection.

Is Freshli free to use?

Freshli's core features are free, including pantry tracking, expiry alerts, basic recipes, and community sharing. Premium features may be available through optional in-app subscriptions.

Troubleshooting

App Not Sending Notifications

Make sure notifications are enabled for Freshli in your iPhone Settings > Notifications > Freshli. Also check that you have notification preferences configured within the app under Settings > Notifications.

Camera Not Working for Scanning

Ensure camera permission is granted in iPhone Settings > Freshli > Camera. If the issue persists, try restarting the app or your device.

Location Features Not Working

Freshli Circles requires location access. Go to iPhone Settings > Freshli > Location and select "While Using the App." Make sure Location Services are enabled globally in Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services.

Syncing Issues

If your data isn't syncing across devices, check your internet connection and ensure you're signed in to the same account. You can force a sync by pulling down on the home screen. If problems persist, contact support@freshli.app.

App Crashing

First, make sure you're running the latest version of Freshli from the App Store. If crashes continue, try deleting and reinstalling the app (your data is synced to the cloud). Please report persistent crashes to support@freshli.app with your device model and iOS version.

Report a Problem

Encountered a bug or issue? Help us improve Freshli by reporting it:

  • In-App: Go to Settings > Help & Feedback > Report a Bug
  • Email: Send details to support@freshli.app

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • A description of what happened
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Screenshots if possible

System Requirements

  • Platform: iOS 18.0 or later
  • Devices: iPhone (all models compatible with iOS 18)
  • Storage: Approximately 80 MB
  • Internet: Required for syncing, community features, and recipe suggestions
  • Permissions: Camera (optional, for scanning), Location (optional, for Circles), Notifications (optional, for alerts)
