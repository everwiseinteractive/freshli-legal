Your Privacy Choices

Control your data and manage your privacy preferences

Your Data, Your Control

At Freshli, we believe you should have complete control over your personal data. This page explains the privacy choices available to you and how to exercise your rights under applicable privacy laws, including the GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and other data protection regulations.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Privacy Rights Available to You

Right Description How to Exercise
Access Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you In-app or email
Correction Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data In-app settings
Deletion Request permanent deletion of your account and all data In-app or email
Portability Receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) In-app or email
Opt-Out Opt out of non-essential data processing and communications In-app settings
Restrict Processing Limit how we process your data Email request
Withdraw Consent Withdraw previously given consent for data processing In-app or device settings

Delete Your Account and Data

You can permanently delete your Freshli account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open Freshli and go to Settings
  2. Tap Account
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your identity (Face ID / Touch ID or password)
  5. Confirm deletion by tapping "Permanently Delete"

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@freshli.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" from the email address associated with your Freshli account. Include your full name and any additional identifying information. We will process your request within 30 days.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account profile and authentication data
  • All pantry items, recipes, and food tracking history
  • Impact metrics and achievement data
  • Freshli Circle memberships and shared listings
  • All messages sent through community features
  • Grocery lists and preferences

Data deletion is completed within 30 days of your request. During this period, your account is deactivated and not visible to other users. Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes as it cannot be linked back to you.

Export Your Data

You can request a complete export of your personal data at any time:

  1. Open Freshli and go to Settings
  2. Tap Privacy
  3. Tap Export My Data
  4. Choose your preferred format (JSON or CSV)
  5. Your data export will be prepared and available for download within 24 hours

Alternatively, email privacy@freshli.app with the subject "Data Export Request".

Your export will include: account information, pantry items and history, recipes and favorites, impact metrics, grocery lists, preferences, and community activity.

Manage Permissions

You can control what data Freshli can access through your device settings:

Location Services

Go to iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Freshli. Choose "Never," "While Using," or "Always." We recommend "While Using" for Circles features. Your exact location is never shared with other users.

Camera Access

Go to iPhone Settings > Freshli > Camera. Toggle off to disable receipt scanning and food label recognition.

Notifications

Go to iPhone Settings > Notifications > Freshli. Customize which notifications you receive, or disable them entirely.

Siri & Shortcuts

Go to iPhone Settings > Freshli > Siri & Search. Control whether Freshli appears in Siri suggestions and search results.

Communication Preferences

You can manage your communication preferences within the app:

  • Push Notifications: Settings > Notifications (customize per category: expiry alerts, community updates, tips)
  • Email Communications: Settings > Notifications > Email Preferences (toggle marketing emails, product updates)
  • In-App Messages: Settings > Notifications > In-App Messages

Note: We will always send essential service communications (security alerts, Terms changes, account notifications) regardless of your preferences.

Do Not Track

Freshli respects "Do Not Track" browser signals and Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. We do not track users across third-party apps or websites. We do not participate in ad networks or share data for targeted advertising.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell or share your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Limit use of sensitive personal information

To submit a verifiable consumer request, email privacy@freshli.app with the subject "CCPA Request". We will verify your identity before processing the request and respond within 45 days.

European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our legal bases for processing are: consent, contract performance, legitimate interests, and legal obligation.

For GDPR-related requests, contact our Data Protection contact at privacy@freshli.app.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests:

  • Privacy Email: privacy@freshli.app
  • General Support: support@freshli.app
  • Address: Freshli, Jay Lawrence, United Kingdom

We respond to all privacy requests within 30 days (or sooner where required by law). You will not be charged a fee for exercising your privacy rights.

Last updated: April 9, 2026
