Why Partner with Freshli?

Freshli users are actively trying to reduce their environmental footprint. They track their food, cook from what they have, share surplus with neighbours, and measure their CO² impact weekly. This is one of the most engaged, values-aligned audiences on the App Store.

Advertise with Freshli

We offer limited, tasteful advertising opportunities for brands that align with our mission. No pop-ups, no tracking, no data selling — just relevant products shown to people who care about sustainability.

What we accept: Eco-conscious food brands, sustainable kitchen products, zero-waste lifestyle brands, organic grocers, plant-based companies.

What we don't: Fast food chains, single-use plastic products, anything that contradicts our mission.
