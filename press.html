FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE — 20 May 2026

Freshli, the AI pantry app turning every UK postcode into a food-rescue community, launches v1.2.0 today.

UK households throw away an estimated £17 billion of edible food every year. Freshli's premise is that most of it goes in the bin because people forget what they have, run out of recipe ideas, and have no friction-free way to give surplus to a neighbour. v1.2.0 closes the last gap — the cold-start problem that kills two-sided marketplaces — by guaranteeing every new user lands in a real, named community area on day one.

What's new in 1.2.0:

  • 118 UK postcode districts pre-seeded as community areas, from Stornoway to Truro, Belfast to Brighton.
  • One-tap "Invite a Neighbour" — both inviter and invitee receive +100 Karma when the friend signs up, and the new user auto-joins the inviter's area.
  • Daily streak engine — opens accumulate, with bonus Karma at 7, 14, 30, 60, 100 and 365 days.
  • 16-tier achievement system unlocking exclusive icons and donations to FareShare UK, Trees for the Future, and The Felix Project.
  • Spotlight pantry search — search "Bananas" from iOS and jump straight into the matched item.
  • Apple Intelligence Writing Tools in the Community listing composer.
  • Siri Shortcut: "Hey Siri, invite a neighbour to Freshli."

Privacy stance: No ads. No third-party tracking. No IDFA. No remote push. Recipes generated entirely on-device via Apple Intelligence — pantry data never leaves the iPhone for AI generation. Council Impact Reports aggregate at the postcode-district level only, fully anonymised, opt-in.

Availability: Free on the App Store for iPhone and iPad running iOS 26 in 11 storefronts: United Kingdom, United States, Canada, Australia, Ireland, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, Netherlands, Brazil, Mexico, Spain. Apple Intelligence features require iPhone 15 Pro or later.

About Freshli

Freshli is an iOS app that helps households rescue food from the bin using on-device Apple Intelligence. Users track their pantry, get AI-generated recipes for expiring ingredients (entirely on-device, with zero network calls), cook hands-free with voice-guided recipe steps, share surplus food with neighbours, and watch their environmental impact grow week by week. Built with Swift 6.2, SwiftUI, SwiftData, and Metal 4 for iOS 26. For the people. For the planet.

What Makes Freshli Different

  • On-device AI: Rescue Chef uses iOS 26's FoundationModels framework — recipes are generated entirely on-device. No server, no API cost, no privacy compromise.
  • Community-first: Share surplus food with neighbours, donate to community fridges, join local pods for hyper-local food sharing.
  • Impact you can feel: Weekly Wrap — a cinematic, shareable story of your food rescue impact with animated MeshGradient backgrounds and a growing tree.
  • Hands-free cooking: Freshli reads every recipe step aloud so users never need to touch their phone while cooking. Eyes on the pan, not the screen.
  • Gaze tracking accessibility: ARKit face tracking enables hands-free browsing — UI elements subtly inflate toward the user's gaze. Full WCAG 2.2 AA compliance.
  • Deep platform integration: Apple Watch, Siri Shortcuts, Control Center widgets, Interactive Widgets, Live Activities, Dynamic Island, and ControlWidget quick actions.
